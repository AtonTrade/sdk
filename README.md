## 🌐 Overview

ATON is a next-generation protocol designed to bring **trust, transparency, and scalability** to AI model evaluation.

By combining **cryptographic commitment schemes** with **peer-to-peer consensus**, ATON ensures that model outputs are verifiable, tamper-proof, and aligned across decentralized participants.

This enables a new paradigm for benchmarking, validating, and governing AI systems in open and distributed environments.

## 🏗️ Architecture

ATON operates through three core layers:

1. **Commitment Layer**  
   - Models submit outputs with cryptographic proofs  
   - Ensures immutability and traceability  

2. **Consensus Layer**  
   - Distributed validators verify results  
   - Achieves agreement via peer-to-peer mechanisms  

3. **Evaluation Layer**  
   - Aggregates scores, rankings, and reputation  
   - Enables standardized benchmarking
